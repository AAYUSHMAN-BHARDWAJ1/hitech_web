About Us

When business works better, the world works better

At Hitech, we are committed to building a better working world — with increased trust and confidence in business, sustainable growth, development of talent in all its forms, and greater collaboration.

Hitech was established in April 2017, and has rapidly built a significant competitive presence in India. The firm operates from its offices at Shillong, Kolkata and Jamshedpur and offers our clients a full range of services, including financial and business advisory, tax and regulatory, and risk advisory services.

Our differentiation is derived from a rapid performance-based, industry-tailored and technology-enabled business advisory services delivered by some of the leading talented professionals in the company.
